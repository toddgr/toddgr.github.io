Gummy bears in Blender!

For this project, I practiced modelling, shading, and rendering in Blender to create a pile of gummy bears. I had a lot of fun working on this project, creating and testing different colors and textures that would look the most like a realistic gummy bear.

If I were to do this project again, I might reduce the specular highlight and increase the roughness, so that the bears look less metallic and shiny. I would also give the bears a deeper base color, so that the illusion of depth with the subsurface scattering is more noticeable.
