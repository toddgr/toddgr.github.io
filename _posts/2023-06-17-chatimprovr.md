I had the amazing opportunity to be part of a group that created a game engine in Rust– from scratch! We called it ChatImproVR, a VR-focused game engine with the goal of creating the ultimate chat and social platform for players to enjoy, with a modular design that would allow any number of games to be integrated within each other. 

<!--more-->

[Visit our GitHub here!](https://chatimprovr.github.io/)

With this game engine that we created, we developed a few mini games that could be played at the Oregon State Engineering Expo 2023. It was a hit! The entire day we had kids, adults, and students alike gathering around our little VR headset area and playing all of our games. I was responsible for creating the assets and designing the computer graphics plugins for the engine. I also was the head designer of the Demo Room game, a game that we showed at the expo which allowed you to navigate a house with different rooms and objects in 3D. If you’re interested in what ChatImproVR is, and the team behind it, I put together this promo video. Take a look!
